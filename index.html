About Me

Engineer at heart. Designer by calling. Leader by practice.

I began crafting pixel-perfect interfaces at IBM and Apple, but quickly realized I was more drawn to how users felt than what tech I used.

I realized I cared more about human connection than technical perfection.

In 2013, I pivoted into product design at VMware, where I shaped 30+ enterprise tools, scaled design systems, and led platform UX across functions.

Today, at Broadcom, I serve as Principal Product Designer — blending strategy, AI, and inclusive design to simplify complex enterprise platforms.

"I design systems that scale, build teams that thrive, and ask better questions to shape what ships."
