The Challenge

Make internal support as delightful as everything else at VMware - a company known for its award-winning culture.

My Role

Lead Product Designer driving UX strategy, research, and design execution across two major platform releases.

Timeline & Team

6 quarters with 3 Designers, 12 Engineers, 3 PMs, and 1 Product Owner.

Efficiency for Fulfillers

Flocks of the Same Feather

Fulfillers struggled with jobs that comprised of multiple, dependent, or sub tasks (think onboarding a new employee or procurement). They had to manually search each ticket to make sense of the progress of the job.

"Now I can see everything in one place. No hunting."
- Field Fulfillment Agent

Form a Meaningful View

I crafted a cohesive UI that brought all related tickets together, improving efficiency by 3x. This prompted the team to ideate a new 'ticket-item' object model to technically design a framework that scaled across departments and allowed solving for dependencies.
